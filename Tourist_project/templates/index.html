Tourist Places Recommendations using Sentiment Analysis

Mumbai

Mumbai, the financial capital of India, is a bustling metropolis that is home to numerous multinational corporations, as well as a thriving film and entertainment industry.

Ahmedabad

Ahmedabad, located in the western state of Gujarat, is famous for its textile industry, as well as historic landmarks such as the Sabarmati Ashram, which was the home of Mahatma Gandhi.

Pune

Pune, a city in Western India, is home to numerous educational institutions, and is known for its youthful vibe, excellent weather, and a range of outdoor activities.

Agra

Agra, located in Northern India, is home to the world-famous Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the Seven Wonders of the World, as well as other historic monuments and landmarks.

Jaipur

Jaipur, the "Pink City" located in the state of Rajasthan, is known for its stunning palaces and forts, vibrant culture, and beautiful handicrafts.

New Delhi

Delhi, the capital of India, is a bustling metropolis that combines the old and new, with numerous historic monuments, bustling markets, and modern infrastructure.

Udaipur

Udaipur, located in the state of Rajasthan, is a city of lakes and palaces, known for its romantic charm, stunning architecture, and rich cultural heritage.

Bangalore

Bangalore, located in Southern India, is known as India's Silicon Valley, as it is home to numerous multinational technology companies, as well as a thriving start-up ecosystem.

Kolkata

Kolkata, the cultural capital of India, is located in Eastern India and is renowned for its colonial architecture, bustling streets, and rich history of art, literature, and music.

Chandigarh

Chandigarh, located in Northern India, is a well-planned city that serves as the capital of both Punjab and Haryana, and is known for its modernist architecture, beautiful gardens, and vibrant cultural scene.

Goa

Goa, located on the western coast of India, is a beach paradise known for its picturesque beaches, laid-back lifestyle, and vibrant nightlife.

Varanasi

Varanasi, situated on the banks of the river Ganges in Northern India, is one of the world's oldest inhabited cities, and is considered to be the spiritual capital of India, attracting millions of pilgrims each year.
